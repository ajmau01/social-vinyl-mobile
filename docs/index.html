Features

Everything the listening room needs.

Built for collectors who actually spin records — not just catalog them.

🎛️

Host a Session

Generate a room code, share it with guests. They join instantly — no account required. Your collection, their discovery.

💿

Live Discogs Collection

Your full collection syncs directly from Discogs. Browse by release, artist, label, or year — right from the phone in anyone's hand.

📥

The Bin

Guests add records to the bin. The host sees what the room wants to hear. A collaborative queue built for vinyl's natural rhythm.

📡

Real-Time, No Refresh

Everything updates live over WebSocket. Bin changes, now playing, session state — all in sync across every device in the room.

🔒

Secure Discogs Auth

Connects via Discogs OAuth — no passwords shared with Social Vinyl. You authorize once and your guests never touch your credentials.

📱

Mobile-First

Designed for the couch, the listening chair, the record fair. Native Android app. iOS coming soon.

How It Works

From crate to room in seconds.

🔗

Link Discogs

Authorize Social Vinyl with your Discogs account via OAuth.

🚀

Start a Session

Tap "Start Party" — get a room code instantly.

👥

Guests Join

Share the code. Guests join with just a display name, no sign-up.

🎶

Browse & Spin

Everyone browses the crate. Guests build the bin. You control the needle.

🏷️

Built on the Discogs database.

Social Vinyl connects directly to your existing Discogs collection — no manual imports, no re-cataloguing. If your records are on Discogs, they're in Social Vinyl. Artwork, pressing details, and all.
